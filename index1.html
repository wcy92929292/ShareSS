Instructions

    1.You need a client to do the next step. You can choose to download the client based on your system. If you already have a client, you can skip to step 3 to continue.
    2.If you have an ss account that you can use, such as your own purchase number or your own server, if you are willing to share it, that's all right, you can fill in the 2 step.
    3.If you don't have an account, you need to click free to randomly get the shared account on the blockchain and continue.
    4.Just click on an image and then it shows a QR code, which is what we need.Or you can click 'Change a Batch' to continue.
    5.You need to open your client and find the server in the right-click menu,then Server -> Share Server Configuration and continue.
    6.Wait a few seconds, the program will locate the QR code on your screen, and then automatically enter the configuration and continue.
    7.When you see the configuration is complete, click OK so that you can use the proxy network.
